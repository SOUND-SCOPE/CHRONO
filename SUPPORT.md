# Support / サポート

## English

### Bug Reports & Feature Requests

Please use **GitHub Issues** for all bug reports and feature requests.

👉 [Open an Issue](../../issues/new)

When reporting a bug, please include:
- Your OS (Windows / macOS / iPadOS)
- Browser and version (e.g. Chrome 124)
- CHRONO edition and version (e.g. LTC Edition β0010.1.0)
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened

### Purchase & Download

CHRONO is available on Gumroad:

👉 [Purchase on Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/) *(link to be added)*

Purchased files can be re-downloaded at any time from your Gumroad purchase history.

### Upgrade Policy

Purchases made during the beta period include a free upgrade to the official release.
When a new version is available, you will be notified by email via Gumroad.
